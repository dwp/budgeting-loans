4. Check your eligibility and apply

You can apply for a Budgeting Loan if you’ve been getting one of these benefits for the past 6 months:

  • Income Support
  • income-based Jobseeker’s Allowance
  • income-related Employment and Support Allowance
  • Pension Credit

Other factors in applying for a Budgeting Loan:

  • you must not owe the Social Fund more than £1,500 for Crisis Loans and Budgeting Loans
  • you must need £100 or more
  • you must not be involved in industrial action (for example, a strike, walkout or lockout)

Online application waiting times

You’ll be told whether your Budgeting Loan has been approved within 12 days. If you choose to accept the terms outlined in your offer, you’ll receive the money within the following 5 days.

Other ways to apply

You can get an application form at your local Jobcentre Plus. If you’d like one posted to you call 0345 603 6967 between 8am and 6pm, Monday to Friday (charges apply).
