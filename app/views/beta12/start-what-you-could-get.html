2. What you could get

You could get up to:

  • £348 if you’re single
  • £464 if you have a partner
  • £812 if you or your partner are claiming Child Benefit

How much you could get depends on whether you:

  • can pay the loan back
  • have savings of more than £1,000 (£2,000 if you or your partner are over 61)
  • owe money to the Social Fund through Budgeting Loans or Crisis Loans
