5. Apply

Check you’re eligible before you apply

Apply online Apply online

If you apply online, you’ll be told whether you’ve been offered a loan within 15 working days. If you accept the offer, you’ll receive the money within 5 working days of your acceptance being received.

The loan is paid into your bank, building society or credit union account.

Other ways to apply

You can print and fill in form SF500. It can take up to 15 working days for your application to be processed.

You can also:

  • pick up the form from your nearest Jobcentre Plus.
  • phone the Social Fund and ask for a form to be posted to you - allow 5 working days for the form to arrive

Social Fund

Telephone: 0345 603 6967

Monday to Friday, 8am to 6pm

Find out about call charges

You can return your completed form by post or hand it in to your local Jobcentre Plus.

Questions about your application

Call the Social Fund if you have a question about the progress of your application.

Allow 15 working days before phoning if you return your form by post (it may not have been processed before then).
