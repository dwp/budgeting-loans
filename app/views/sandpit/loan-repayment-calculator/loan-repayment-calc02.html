3. Paying back the loan

A Budgeting Loan is interest free so you only pay back what you borrow. The loan repayments are based on how much benefit you get and what you can afford.

The repayments will automatically be taken from your benefits.

You normally have to repay the loan within 2 years (104 weeks). If you stop getting benefits, youâ€™ll need to arrange another way to repay.
